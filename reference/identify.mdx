---
title: "Identify Visitors"
description: "Identify visitors by associating them with a unique identifier. This helps track user behavior across sessions and devices."
openapi: "POST /identify"
---

<Note>
This endpoint allows you to identify visitors by associating them with a unique identifier. This helps track user behavior across sessions and devices. Please include at least one of: userId, externalId, or email to properly identify the user.
</Note>
