# The Trade Desk

The Trade Desk is a leading programmatic advertising platform that empowers marketers to reach audiences across display, video, audio, native, and connected TV channels. With this destination, you will be able to send conversion events to The Trade Desk for unified attribution, advanced targeting, and optimization across the open internet.

## Overview

The Ours integration with The Trade Desk enables you to track conversions and other key events through a secure, server-to-server integration. By routing all events through our servers, we ensure that sensitive data remains protected while accurately tracking your advertising performance.

The Trade Desk provides advanced programmatic advertising capabilities across display, video, audio, native, and connected TV channels, allowing for unified attribution and optimization across the open internet.

## Configuration

### Destination Setup

1. Navigate to **Destinations** in your Ours platform
2. Search for **The Trade Desk** and add it to your destinations
3. Configure the mapping fields as needed for your use case

## Mapping Fields

The Trade Desk destination provides comprehensive mapping options for various types of event data. Here are a subset of the available fields you can configure:

* **Event Name**: The name of the conversion event (e.g., "purchase", "signup", "lead")
* **Value**: The monetary value of the conversion for revenue tracking and ROI measurement
* **Currency**: The currency code for the conversion value
* **Client IP**: The IP address of the user for attribution purposes
* **URL**: The URL where the conversion event occurred

The mapping configuration from your dashboard contains tooltips that describe and contain more detailed information about each field.
